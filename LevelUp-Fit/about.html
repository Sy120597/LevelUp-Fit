  • 🎯 Set fitness goals tailored to your lifestyle and unlock visual milestones as you progress.

Privacy & Data Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Any data you enter stays on your device unless you choose to connect with our server. We do not track or share your activity and progress without your permission.

By using this platform, you agree to our responsible data handling practices and, upon opting in, consent to receive motivational communications.
